Magazine

I have worked on the school newspaper-turned-magazine since freshman year. I was a part of the transition to a magazine format in my role as a layout editor. This year I am going to be a managing editor as well, running each aspect of the magazine alongside the rest of the editorial board.

Baking

cake

I love to make cakes for any events that my family has. Birthday parties and Easter celebrations feature cakes made by me. I also love to make cakes for fun when the inspiration strikes. I am inspired by baking competitions and shows on TV as well as new ideas I see online.

cookies

I love making cookies to share with my firends and family. They are the perfect thing to make on a normal day to brighten up the moods of the people around me and have something to share with them.

Travel

Over the summer I traveled to the Dominican Republic. It was a leap out of my comfort zone, because I had never been on a plane before or even left New England. I went there to do service and learn about NGOs. I learned so much and had a lot of fun.
